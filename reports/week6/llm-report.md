# LLM Report — Week 6

## 1. Application AI/LLM Integration

Our project continues to use LLM capabilities as a core feature for food recommendation.

- **Model Implementation:** The `ai_service.py` module remains in place, supporting stub, OpenAI, and LM Studio backends.
- **Purpose:** LLMs are used to process user preferences (allergies, dietary restrictions, likes, dislikes) and recommend dishes based on the uploaded menu and user input.
- **Prompt Engineering:** Custom system prompts continued to be refined to ensure the AI returns valid JSON recommendations that respect user constraints such as budget, exclusions, and mood/craving input.
- **Safety Hardening:** This sprint, we implemented code-enforced guarantees that excluded ingredients (allergies, dislikes, and plain-language exclusions) are never recommended, regardless of the AI backend. An LLM-backed supplement was added to catch exclusion phrasings missed by the original regex-based parser, itself guarded against hallucinating an exclusion on a purely positive message.

## 2. Development & Productivity Usage

- **Coding:** AI was used to help generate parts of the frontend and backend implementation (order history, dislike feature, AI safety hardening) and to fix bugs. AI assistance was also used to debug deployment issues.
- **Transcription:** AI was used to generate a transcript of the customer meeting, which was then sanitized and reviewed by the team.
- **Writing:** AI was used to reformulate sentences in reports to make them clearer and more concise. Some report content was converted to Markdown format for better structure and readability.
- **Research & Idea Generation:** AI was consulted for advice on the website's interface design and color scheme. The team used AI to better understand tasks and explore possible approaches before implementing them independently.
- **Understanding Tasks:** The team used AI to better understand assignment requirements and explore possible approaches before implementing them independently.

## 3. Usage Policy

All content generated by AI was carefully reviewed, edited, and validated by team members before inclusion. The assignment deliverables do not contain any AI-generated content in its original, unmodified form.
