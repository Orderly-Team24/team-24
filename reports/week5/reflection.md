**Week 5 Reflection**

**Learning points**
* Got more experience with building login and registration flows and connecting them to the backend.
* Learned that features that depend on each other work better when developed together, not one after another.
* Learned how to work with multiple deployed services and fix issues that come up along the way.

**Validated assumptions**
* Confirmed that the app stays usable even when some features are not fully ready yet.
* Confirmed that customer feedback early on helps avoid reworking things later.
 
**Friction and gaps**
* Some features were hard to test because they depended on other parts that weren't ready yet.
* Fixing deployment issues took more time than expected.

**Planned response**
* Talk more often with teammates working on related features to catch problems early.
* Make sure the live version of the app is working before the sprint ends.
* Keep improving the app based on what the customer says.
